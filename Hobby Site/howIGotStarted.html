When

I started playing volleyball when I was in seventh grade. This is when most boys start playing volleyball, because there are no teams at any of the younger ages. Girls can start as young as 4th grade.

Where

I started playing volleyball at Kettle Moraine Middle School. My coach was Tom Gulak. When I started I was a setter

Why

I started playing volleyball because my Mom(above) got me started. She was an All-State volleyball player for Wauwatosa East when she was in highschool.
